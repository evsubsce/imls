Welcome to the EVSU Interactive Learning System

The EVSU Interactive Learning System (EVSU-ILS) is a web-based educational platform developed by the Civil Engineering Department of Eastern Visayas State University. It provides interactive multimedia instructional materials designed to enhance classroom instruction and independent learning.

Each course is carefully developed to integrate lesson notes, animations, worked examples, interactive exercises, quizzes, engineering calculators, and other learning resources into a single, mobile-friendly platform that students can access anytime and anywhere.
